My Health App

This is a personal project that uses Fitbit Web API to access and visualize health data such as sleep, heart rate, and activity metrics. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Fitbit or Google.

Privacy Policy

Your Fitbit data is used exclusively on your device or personal server and is not shared with third parties. No data is stored on any external servers. OAuth tokens are stored locally and securely. This app respects your privacy.
